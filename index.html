Madison Bike Count Visualization

Madison has very cold weather which often makes biking challenging, but it has wonderful bike trails. When would people be more likely to use them? I want to find out the answer from two bike counters located at North Shore Drive and Monroe Street respectively. This application allows users to visualize bike traffic count for the entire year of 2016.
